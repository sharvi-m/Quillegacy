Our Product

A personalized memory booklet, made one story at a time.

Our product is a personalized memory booklet, made up of around fifteen adaptable questions for each individual. To account for memory loss, questions can easily be exchanged or omitted.

It is meant not only as a self-anchor, but as a way of connecting the past to the present and the future.

Interview Questions

A starting point — questions we use to draw out the moments, lessons, and memories that matter most.

  1. What advice or wisdom would you want to pass down to future generations?
  2. What is your favorite hobby or pastime?
  3. What did you imagine your career path would be when you were younger?
  4. Who were your idols in your life?
  5. Tell us a story about a challenge you overcame that made you stronger.
  6. What is the innovation or invention created during your lifetime that impacted you most?
  7. When you were a kid, did you get in trouble a lot, and what was a time you did something very mischievous?
  8. What did you do as a career, and what did you enjoy most about it?
  9. Do you have a personal motto or philosophy you live by?
  10. What is your favorite place you have traveled, and why was it so memorable?
  11. What is your proudest accomplishment?
  12. What is your fondest or most vivid childhood memory?
  13. Do you have any traditions, and if so, what are they?
  14. How would people you love describe you, and how would you describe yourself?
  15. Where was your childhood home, and what high school and college did you attend?
  16. What makes you happy, and what do you value the most?
