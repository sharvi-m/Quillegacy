About Us

Why we started Quillegacy.

Our mission is to help seniors pass their memories and wisdom down to their families and future generations through a carefully curated and personalized memory booklet.

The first step to making the memory booklet is to interview the seniors about their best memories and the lessons they have learned throughout their lives. We preserve the stories that would otherwise be forgotten. These sessions not only help families remember their loved ones — reminiscing also brings joy and comfort to the seniors themselves.

Through carefully customized and thought-out designs, experiences from the past come to life once more.
