Who We Are

The team behind Quillegacy.

We are three 8th graders who believe every senior carries a story worth remembering — and that no one's memories should be lost to time.

Quillegacy began as a small idea between friends and grew into a project we care deeply about. We're learning, listening, and building something meaningful — one interview, one booklet, and one life story at a time.
