Our Service

From an interview to a handcrafted booklet.

Through a thirty-minute interview between each individual and a member of Quillegacy, a book is created. The answers are recorded and written down, then thoughtfully handcrafted into a meaningful booklet.

What we value

The four ideas that guide every booklet we make.

Self Identity

Residents have a way to remember themselves and hold on to their identity while experiencing memory loss.

Remembrance

Families have a tangible book to remember and cherish their loved ones.

Connection

Both elderly people and younger generations can connect with each other.

Wisdom

Students have the opportunity to learn from the experiences of the seniors.

Quillegacy

Preserving memories. Honoring lives. Connecting generations.
