About Us

Our mission is to help seniors pass their memories and wisdom down to their families and future generations through a carefully curated and personalized memory booklet.

The first step to making the memory booklet is to interview the seniors about their best memories and the lessons they have learned throughout their lives. We preserve the stories that would otherwise be forgotten. Through carefully customized and thought-out designs, experiences from the past come to life once more.

Impact

A memory booklet is more than a keepsake — it's a bridge between generations.

For Families

Loved ones gain a lasting record of the people, places, and lessons that shaped their family's story — something to read, share, and pass down.

For Seniors

Reminiscing brings joy and comfort. Our interview sessions give seniors a meaningful space to revisit and celebrate the moments that matter most.

For Future Generations

Stories that would otherwise be forgotten are preserved — so grandchildren and great-grandchildren can know the voices of the people who came before.

Who We Are

We are three 8th graders who believe every senior carries a story worth remembering — and that no one's memories should be lost to time.

Quillegacy began as a small idea between friends and grew into a project we care deeply about. We're learning, listening, and building something meaningful — one interview, one booklet, and one life story at a time.
